## Password Requirements

Your password must meet the following security requirements:

### ‚úÖ Required Elements
- **Minimum 8 characters** long
- **At least 1 uppercase letter** (A-Z)
- **At least 1 lowercase letter** (a-z)
- **At least 1 number** (0-9)
- **At least 1 special character** (!@#$%^&*(),.?":{}|<>)

### ‚ùå Restrictions
- Cannot be the same as your current password
- Cannot be any of your last 5 passwords
- Cannot contain common patterns or dictionary words

### üí° Password Strength Indicator
The system provides a real-time password strength meter that shows:
- **Weak** (Red): Missing multiple requirements
- **Fair** (Orange): Missing 1-2 requirements
- **Good** (Yellow): Meets most requirements
- **Strong** (Green): Meets all requirements

## Changing Your Password

### Method 1: Through Security Settings

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Navigate** to Profile ‚Üí Security Settings
3. **Click** on the "Password" tab
4. **Click** "Change Password" button
5. **Fill in the form**:
   - Enter your current password
   - Enter your new password
   - Confirm your new password
6. **Click** "Change Password" to save

### Method 2: Through Profile Menu

1. **Login** to your account
2. **Click** on your profile picture/name
3. **Select** "Security Settings"
4. **Follow** steps 4-6 from Method 1

### üîí Security Features
- **Current password verification** - You must enter your current password
- **Real-time validation** - Instant feedback on password strength
- **Password history check** - Prevents reusing recent passwords
- **Secure transmission** - All data is encrypted during transmission

## Forgot Password

If you forget your password, you can reset it safely:

### Step 1: Request Password Reset

1. **Go** to the login page
2. **Click** "Forgot password?" link
3. **Enter** your email address
4. **Click** "Send Reset Link"
5. **Check** your email for the reset link

### Step 2: Reset Your Password

1. **Click** the link in your email
2. **Enter** your new password
3. **Confirm** your new password
4. **Click** "Reset Password"
5. **Login** with your new password

### üõ°Ô∏è Security Notes
- Reset links expire after **15 minutes**
- Links can only be used **once**
- You'll receive an email confirmation after successful reset
- If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder

## Best Practices

### üîê Creating Strong Passwords

1. **Use a unique password** for your membership account
2. **Combine random words** with numbers and symbols
3. **Avoid personal information** (names, birthdays, addresses)
4. **Use a password manager** to generate and store passwords
5. **Don't share your password** with anyone

### üõ°Ô∏è Account Security

1. **Change passwords regularly** (every 90 days recommended)
2. **Monitor your account activity** regularly
3. **Log out from shared computers** completely
4. **Use secure networks** when accessing your account
5. **Report suspicious activity** immediately

### üì± Mobile Security

1. **Use device lock screens** (PIN, fingerprint, face unlock)
2. **Don't save passwords** in public browsers
3. **Log out after use** on mobile devices
4. **Keep your app updated** to the latest version

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Current password is incorrect"
- **Solution**: Double-check your current password
- **Tip**: Use the "Show password" button to verify what you're typing
- **Alternative**: Use the "Forgot password" feature if you can't remember

#### "Password does not meet requirements"
- **Solution**: Check the password strength indicator
- **Tip**: Each missing requirement is listed below the password field
- **Example**: "Password must contain at least one uppercase letter"

#### "Cannot reuse recent passwords"
- **Solution**: Choose a password you haven't used recently
- **Info**: The system remembers your last 5 passwords
- **Tip**: Use a password manager to generate unique passwords

#### "Too many login attempts"
- **Solution**: Wait 15 minutes before trying again
- **Info**: This protects your account from brute force attacks
- **Alternative**: Use the "Forgot password" feature

#### "Password reset link expired"
- **Solution**: Request a new password reset link
- **Info**: Reset links expire after 15 minutes for security
- **Tip**: Complete the reset process immediately after receiving the email

### Getting Help
